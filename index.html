Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

Sudharsan Engineering College was established in 2000 under the Sudharsan Educational Trust, located at Sathiyamangalam on NH 210 — about 12 km from Pudukkottai and 40 km from Trichy. Founded with a commitment to providing quality technical education in the rural district of Pudukkottai.

Affiliated to Anna University Chennai and approved by AICTE, the college holds a prestigious NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation — reflecting its commitment to academic standards, infrastructure, and student outcomes.
